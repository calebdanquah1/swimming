About your instructor

Hey there! I’m Benedicta, your dedicated private swim instructor serving Mississauga, Toronto, and nearby areas. Whether you're just getting started, refining your technique, or jumping into swimming for fitness, I’m here to support you in reaching your goals. With over 14 years of experience, I’ve honed my skills and built a strong foundation in swim instruction. I’m a certified National Lifeguard, fully trained in First Aid and CPR, so safety is always a top priority. From young children to adults, I’ve had the pleasure of guiding swimmers of all ages as they gain confidence and dive into their swimming journey. Known for my calm and patient teaching style, I focus on creating a relaxed, encouraging space where every swimmer can thrive. I enjoy building personal connections, recognizing each person’s unique strengths and challenges, and working together to reach meaningful progress. What makes my lessons unique? It’s my sincere commitment to my students' growth and confidence. There’s nothing more rewarding than seeing someone finally master a skill they’ve been working on – it’s those lightbulb moments that inspire me every day. My thoughtful and supportive approach helps foster a positive, empowering experience in the water. So, if you're ready for an engaging and supportive swim journey, let’s make a splash together!
